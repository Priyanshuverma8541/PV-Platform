## Risk Management

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|-----------|
| MongoDB Atlas downtime | Low | High | Multi-region backup |
| Cloudinary rate limits | Low | Medium | Caching layer |
| Gemini API rate limits | Medium | High | Local model fallback |
| Security vulnerability | Low | Critical | Regular audits, bug bounty |
| Team bandwidth | Medium | High | Automated testing, CI/CD |

## Key Decisions Made

✅ **Start with Gemini** - Free, powerful, widely available  
✅ **Monorepo structure** - Shared code, unified deployment  
✅ **MongoDB + Cloudinary** - Free tiers, scalable  
✅ **Vercel + Render** - Free deployments  
✅ **All configurable** - No hardcoding  
✅ **Full automation** - No manual processes
